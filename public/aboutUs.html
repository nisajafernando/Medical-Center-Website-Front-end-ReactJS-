About Us

   Who We Are

St. Anne’s Medical Centre has been a trusted name in Ganemulla Sri Lankan healthcare for more than two Decades. Since our foundation in 2000, we have built a reputation for regional leadership in medical excellence and innovation, based on a simple philosophy: that improving the health of our community should be driven by passion as well as compassion.
